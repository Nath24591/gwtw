How to enter the prize competitions

1. Pick the Competition you want to enter.
2. Choose the number(s) you would like in the draw.
3. Answer the qualifying question (answer must be correct for your entry to be counted)
4. Complete the payment.
5. You will receive a entry email with your number(s) in the draw

I have forgotten what my ticket numbers

- You will have received an email verifying your order.
- You can login and check “Profile” > “Entries” where you will see all your entries

How winners are picked

Once all tickets are sold, our algorithm will pick a random ticket out of all the entries and inform the winner via email.

Can i have a cash alternative?

When claiming your prize, you will be given the option for the prize you won OR 80% RRP of the item in cash.

How do i claim my prize

If you are a lucky winner you can log in and claim your prize from "Profile" > "Entries"

Is GoneWithTheWin a scam?

GoneWithTheWin was foundered in June 2020 and is a fully registered UK based limited company. We are fully compliant and run prize draws which abide by the Gambling act of 2005’s guidelines.

Our goal here at GoneWithTheWin is to make great prizes available to everyone!
